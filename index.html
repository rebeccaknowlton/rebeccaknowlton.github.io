Michael R. Schwob

Bio

Michael Schwob is a doctoral student in the Department of Statistics and Data Sciences at the University of Texas at Austin. His research focuses on agent-based models, spatiotemporal statistics, infectious disease dynamics, quantitative ecology, and biostatistics. Michael was named a two-time Goldwater Scholar and NSF Graduate Research Fellow.
